About SoundCloud Download Gating By BandPass Records

Create private SoundCloud-gated download links for your fans

Our Mission

SoundCloud Download Gating By BandPass Records helps artists and labels grow engagement while delivering downloads in a simple, private way. You share a link, fans complete the SoundCloud requirements, and then unlock the download.

Gates are designed to be non-discoverable: there is no public browsing directory. Only people with your link can access the gate.

What We Do

Easy upload

Create a gate and upload your audio file in a dedicated step with progress.

Share a private link

Post the gate link anywhere. There’s no public browse page—only your fans with the link can access it.

Gated verification

Require like/comment/follow (including optional additional profiles) before the download is unlocked.

Secure downloads

Uploaded files are protected in production and served via the authenticated download endpoint.

Key Features

  • Two-step creation: Create the gate first, then upload/replace the file with progress
  • SoundCloud verification: Like/comment/follow requirements re-check on refresh
  • Collab support: Require following additional SoundCloud profiles
  • Private by design: No public listing/browse—share only via link
  • Creator dashboard: Manage your gates and see downloads
  • Owner notifications: Email alerts with title + total downloads
